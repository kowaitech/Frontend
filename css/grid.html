  • gap ‚Üí space between boxes (rows & columns).

  • column-gap ‚Üí space only between columns.

  • justify-items ‚Üí moves items left, right, center inside their column.
  • align-items ‚Üí moves items top, bottom, middle inside their row.
  • place-items ‚Üí shorthand for both.

- justify-items: Like pushing boxes left, right or centering them in the column. - align-items: Like lifting boxes up, dropping them down, or keeping them in the middle. - place-items: Write both in one go.
  • justify-content ‚Üí moves the whole grid left, right, or spaces it out horizontally.
