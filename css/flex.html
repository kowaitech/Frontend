Flex Container: display

  • Applies to container; sets element as a flex container.
  • Real-life: A tray arranging items in a line automatically.
Code:
<div style="display: flex;"> <div>Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div>Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Container: flex-direction

  • Applies to container; sets main axis direction (row, row-reverse, column, column-reverse).
  • Real-life: Arrange items left-to-right, right-to-left, or top-to-bottom.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: column;"> <div>Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div>Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Container: flex-wrap

  • Applies to container; allows items to wrap onto multiple lines.
  • Values: nowrap (default), wrap, wrap-reverse.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; flex-wrap: wrap; width: 250px;"> <div>Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div>Item 3</div> <div>Item 4</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3
Item 4

Flex Container: flex-flow

  • Applies to container; shorthand for flex-direction + flex-wrap.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; flex-flow: column wrap;"> <div>Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div>Item 3</div> <div>Item 4</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3
Item 4

Flex Container: justify-content

  • Applies to container; aligns items along main axis.
  • Values: flex-start, flex-end, center, space-between, space-around, space-evenly.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; justify-content: space-between;"> <div>Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div>Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Container: align-items

  • Applies to container; aligns items along cross axis.
  • Values: stretch, flex-start, flex-end, center, baseline.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-end; height: 100px;"> <div style="height: 30px;">Item 1</div> <div style="height: 50px;">Item 2</div> <div style="height: 40px;">Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Container: align-content

  • Applies to container; aligns rows when flex-wrap is enabled.
  • Values: flex-start, flex-end, center, space-between, space-around, stretch.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; flex-wrap: wrap; align-content: space-between; height: 150px;"> <div style="flex: 1 0 100px;">Item 1</div> <div style="flex: 1 0 100px;">Item 2</div> <div style="flex: 1 0 100px;">Item 3</div> <div style="flex: 1 0 100px;">Item 4</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3
Item 4

Flex Item: order

  • Applies to item; controls the order of items (default: 0).
  • Real-life: Reordering items on a tray.
Code:
<div style="display: flex;"> <div style="order: 2;">Item 1</div> <div style="order: 1;">Item 2</div> <div style="order: 3;">Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Item: flex-grow / flex-shrink / flex-basis

  • flex-grow: How much item grows.
  • flex-shrink: How much item shrinks.
  • flex-basis: Initial size before growing/shrinking.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; width: 400px;"> <div style="flex: 1 1 100px;">Item 1</div> <div style="flex: 2 1 100px;">Item 2</div> <div style="flex: 1 1 100px;">Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Item: flex

  • Applies to item; shorthand for flex-grow, flex-shrink, flex-basis.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; width: 400px;"> <div style="flex: 1;">Item 1</div> <div style="flex: 2;">Item 2</div> <div style="flex: 1;">Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3

Flex Item: align-self

  • Applies to item; overrides container's align-items for a single item.
  • Real-life: One product at a different height than others.
Code:
<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; height: 100px;"> <div style="align-self: center;">Item 1</div> <div>Item 2</div> <div style="align-self: flex-end;">Item 3</div> </div>
Output Preview:
Item 1
Item 2
Item 3
